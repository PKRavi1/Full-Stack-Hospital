About Ravi's Healthcare

  1. Ravi's Healthcare is a joint venture initiative between KOS S.P.A. Italy and Clearview Healthcare India.
  2. Ravi's Healthcare is a rapidly expanding healthcare services organisation, providing excellent healthcare treatments across India.
  3. With over 4 independent Super specialty Hospitals in Delhi NCR and Mysore region of India and 15 partner hospitals focussed on Oncology and tertiary care.
  4. Ravi's Healthcare aims to become a leading healthcare organisation and cater to the underserved sections of society as well.

Ravi's Healthcare India

  • Ravi's has been in the business of managing complex clinical services and equipment needs of a hospital.
  • We do the design, development and management of complex medical equipment needs of hospitals and private/public healthcare facilities.
  • Ravi's has been vendor neutral and thus deploys equipment from all the suppliers in consultation with the hospital.
  • From managing a particular department we have now further progressed, and have moved into managing the entire running and operations of full-fledged hospital services.
  • Considering the dynamics involved in India every hospital acts as a unique set-up and we have been able to achieve a good working.
  • partnership model with close to some 15 hospitals in India and our partners are rapidly growing.
