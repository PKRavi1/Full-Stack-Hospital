Our International Patient Services includes a holistic package including your treatment plan, visa assistance, airport transfers,
translators and hotel reservations. We also help in coordinating doctor appointments.

A special patient care services team is assigned for helping in flight and hotels reservations and local transfers and taxis.

A lounge is offered to patients and their families for meeting other international patients and feeling right at home!

A highly qualified and experienced team of doctors and specialists at Ravi's Healthcare ensures a high success rate and high patient satisfaction.
We offer over 27+ specialised and general medicine departments. Our team of medical consultants work in unity
to ensure a truly international level of patient care in a friendly environment focussed at ensuring rapid healing and recovery.

Ravi's Healthcare has been accredited by various national accreditation bodies. This is a result of the laser-sharp focus to achieve the highest levels of patient care at Ravi's Healthcare.
